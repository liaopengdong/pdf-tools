Privacy Policy

BayMaxHome PDF provides browser-based PDF tools. For tools marked as local browser processing, selected files are processed in the visitor's browser and are not intentionally uploaded to a BayMaxHome server.

Information collected

The site may collect standard analytics information such as device type, browser type, approximate location, referring page, and pages visited. This data helps maintain and improve the site.

Files

Browser-based tools process files locally through client-side JavaScript libraries. Visitors should still avoid using public or shared devices for confidential documents.

Advertising and analytics

The site may use analytics, advertising, and essential technical cookies subject to applicable law and third-party policies.
