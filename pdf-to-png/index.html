FAQ

Is PNG better than JPG? PNG is lossless and often better for text, screenshots, and graphics. JPG is usually smaller for photo-heavy pages.

Why is the output a ZIP? Multi-page PDFs produce multiple PNG files, so ZIP keeps them together.
