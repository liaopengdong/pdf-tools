PDF to JPG vs screenshot: which is better?

A screenshot captures what is visible on your screen. PDF to JPG conversion renders each page directly, which is usually more consistent for multi-page documents.

Use PDF to JPG when

  • You need every page exported.
  • You want consistent page dimensions.
  • You need images for previews or uploads.

Use screenshots when

Screenshots are fine for one quick visual capture, especially when you only need part of a page.
