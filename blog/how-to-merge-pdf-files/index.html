How to merge PDF files online

Merging PDF files is useful when separate documents belong together: contracts and attachments, invoice batches, scanned forms, or exported reports. A merged PDF is easier to send, archive, and review.

Steps

  1. Open the merge PDF tool.
  2. Select the files in the order you want them combined.
  3. Process the files and download the merged PDF.

When to merge PDFs

Merge files when the reader should move through them as one document. If the recipient only needs one section, extracting pages may be better.
